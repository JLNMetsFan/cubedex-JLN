To get started with Cubedex:

  1. Connect your smart cube by clicking the Connect button. For more details, refer to the FAQ.
  2. Load an algorithm from the list or input your own in the input field using standard Rubik's cube notation. You can also input the algorithm by performing it on the cube.
  3. Ensure the cube is oriented with WHITE on top and GREEN on front.
  4. Click the Train button to start practicing the algorithm.

During training:

  • The timer will start automatically when you begin the algorithm.
  • Upon successful completion, the timer will stop and your last 5 times along with their average will be displayed.
  • If you make a mistake, follow the moves shown in the Fix section to correct it. Ensure the top center is WHITE and the front center is GREEN.
